 NSCMI-2008

XX International Workshop on Neutron Scattering in Condensed Matter Investigations (NSCMI-2008), Gatchina, 13-19 October, 2008.

 Organized by

the Petersburg Nuclear Physics Institute the named after B. P. Konstantinov of the Russian Academy of Sciences


 Sponsored by

the Ministry of Education and Science of the Russian Federation, the Russian Foundation for Basic Research (RFBR), the Dynasty Foundation


 Participants

Russian and invited foreign speakers


 Topics

The Workshop scientific program will include lectures by invited speakers, with parallel oral and poster sessions covering the following fields:

  • crystal structure and elementary excitations
  • magnetism, non-crystal materials
  • nanomaterials, pharmacology and biology
  • science of materials and applied research
  • experimental methods and techniques
  • fundamental properties of neutron

 Registration

In order to get financial support for the Workshop and in accordance with RFBR rules, the organizing committee must urgently submit to RFBR a demand including the scientific program (a detailed description of subjects of each session with speaker’s names and a list of talks).

For working out such a tentative program, the candidates are strongly invited to present their registration forms with titles and short annotations (up to 5-10 lines) of their talks before 15 May, 2008. The final version of the Program will include all corrections and additions of the participants.


 Abstract Submission

Abstracts should be uploaded as Microsoft Word files on NSCMI-2008 Web Site not later than 1 June, 2008, or submitted by e-mail to Yury Chetverikov ( Yurii.Chetverikovpnpi.spb.ru). Please limit your abstract to 1 page (up to 3000 symbols including gaps, all margins – 20 mm, the font used is Times-New-Roman-12).

The Workshop proceedings are planned to be published in a special issue of the journal "Physics of the Solid State . The recommended size for each presentation is limited to 12000 symbols including gaps. The format is determined by the recommendations of the journal. Authors are invited to send their presentations before 13 October, 2008. It will be also possible to submit a presentation upon arrival.
